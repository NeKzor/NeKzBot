|||
|---|---|

## .portal2.map \<map_name... = optional\>

Posts an overview image of a give map. A random map will be chosen if no name is provided.

|||
|---|---|
|Rate Limit|6 Times in 1 Minute|
|Lifetime|5 Minutes|
